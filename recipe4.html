Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Granola
  • Honey
  • Fresh berries

Preparation Time:

5 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Layer Greek yogurt, granola, and fresh berries in a glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle honey on top for sweetness.
  3. Repeat the layering process, ending with a topping of berries.
  4. Serve immediately as a healthy and delicious snack or breakfast option.
