Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • Assorted fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, oranges, berries)
  • Honey (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Preparation Time:

10 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Combine the fruits in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with honey and lemon juice for added flavor (optional).
  4. Toss gently to mix everything together.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for a few minutes before serving.
