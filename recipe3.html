Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Preparation Time:

5 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado on the toasted bread slices.
  4. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes for a kick (optional).
  5. Serve immediately.
