Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Preparation Time:

10 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the pan.
  4. Add the cheese slices on top of the bread in the pan.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Remove from the pan, slice, and serve hot.
